About PictsManager

PictsManager originally started as a 4th-year student project. At the beginning of 2026, I decided to take over the project and significantly improve it, with the ambition of turning it into a more mature and production-ready application.

The project now focuses on clean architecture, modern development practices, scalability, and long-term maintainability. It serves both as a learning platform and a technical showcase of my approach to software engineering.
